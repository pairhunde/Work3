About Us

WE ARE MARG EDUCATION!!!

MARG Education is a leading educational and consultancy company assisting its clients (individuals, businesses, organizations) with planning for higher education, training, and business process management. We specialize in securing admission into universities at various levels of study for individuals who are interested in studying outside of their countries of origin. We have a good working relationship with many world class institutions which include but not limited to institutions in the United Kingdom, United States of America, Canada, and Australia

OUR MISSION

We are delighted in providing consulting services to both individuals seeking admission into foreign institutions of higher learning and businesses seeking to improve productivity through human capacity development. Our main objectives are:

  • Ensure students are placed quickly in reputable foreign institutions
  • Provide top notch counselling for students before they depart for their study destination
  • Assist students and travelers with visa application and acquisition process
  • Train and direct both governmental and non-governmental organizations on the process of sponsoring students abroad
  • Train employees of businesses and organizations in the most relevant management and technology processes.

OUR VALUES

We maintain strong integrity and provide excellent customer service which has earned us great reputation whereby 99.98% of students that apply to foreign institutions through us get admitted to their institutions of choice. The following values guide us in providing the best service we can.:

  • Customer first
  • Integrity
  • Professionalism
  • Honesty
